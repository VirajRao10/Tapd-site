Promotional Text
Control Anki Desktop reviews from your Apple Watch over local Wi-Fi, with no account, cloud sync, ads, or analytics.
Description
Tapd is a standalone Apple Watch controller for Anki Desktop. Pair with the Tapd Anki add-on on your computer, then show answers, grade cards, undo, bury, and suspend cards from your wrist. Choose Button, Minimal, Crown, Double Tap, or Gesture Mode for the review style that fits your session. Tapd runs on your local Wi-Fi network with no phone companion app, account, cloud backend, analytics, ads, or paid API.

Privacy Answers

Suggested App Privacy selection: Data Not Collected. Tapd stores settings on the user's Watch and computer and sends commands only to the user's local Anki add-on. There is no developer-operated server, analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDK.

Export Compliance

Suggested answer: Tapd does not implement proprietary encryption or use HTTPS/TLS. It uses local HTTP and Apple Keychain storage for the pairing token. Confirm this in App Store Connect based on your final binary and legal obligations.

Review Notes

Tapd requires the companion Tapd Anki add-on for Anki Desktop. To test, install the Tapd add-on from AnkiWeb, restart Anki, open Tools -> Tapd Watch Controller, start a review session, then pair the Watch app using the displayed local IP address, port, and 6-digit pairing code.
