Promotional Text
Set up Anki pairing on iPhone, then control Anki Desktop reviews from Apple Watch over local Wi-Fi.
Description
Tapd is an iPhone and Apple Watch controller for Anki Desktop. Use the iPhone app to enter the local IP, port, and pairing code from the Tapd Anki add-on, then send those details to Apple Watch and control reviews from your wrist. Choose Button, Minimal, Crown, Double Tap, or Gesture Mode for the review style that fits your session. Tapd runs on your local Wi-Fi network with no account, cloud backend, analytics, ads, or paid API.

Privacy Answers

Suggested App Privacy selection: Data Not Collected. Tapd stores setup settings on the user's iPhone, Watch, and computer and sends commands only to the user's local Anki add-on. There is no developer-operated server, analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDK.

Export Compliance

Suggested answer: Tapd does not implement proprietary encryption or use HTTPS/TLS. It uses local HTTP and Apple Keychain storage for the pairing token. Confirm this in App Store Connect based on your final binary and legal obligations.

Review Notes

Tapd requires the Tapd iPhone app, Tapd Apple Watch app, and the companion Tapd Anki add-on for Anki Desktop. To test, install the Tapd add-on from AnkiWeb, restart Anki, open Tools -> Tapd Watch Controller, start a review session, enter the displayed local IP address, port, and 6-digit pairing code in the iPhone app, then tap Send to Watch and open Tapd on Apple Watch.

Tapd runs locally on your network. Home
