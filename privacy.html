Tapd
How To Support Privacy

Effective May 15, 2026

Privacy Policy

Tapd is designed to work locally between your iPhone, Apple Watch, and the Tapd Anki add-on running on your computer.

Summary

Tapd does not collect, sell, track, or share personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, third-party tracking SDKs, accounts, cloud sync, or a hosted backend.

Data Stored on Your Devices

The iPhone app stores the computer IP address, port, and Watch device name so it can send setup details to your Apple Watch. The Apple Watch app stores the computer IP address, port, selected controller mode, device name, and pairing token so the app can reconnect to your Anki add-on. The pairing token is stored in the Apple Watch Keychain. The Anki add-on stores its local bridge settings, current pairing code, paired device name, and pairing token in Anki's add-on configuration on your computer.

iPhone to Watch Sync

When you tap Send to Watch, Tapd sends the IP address, port, pairing code, and device name from the iPhone app to the Watch app using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This setup message is used to complete local pairing and is not sent to Tapd servers.

Local Network Communication

Tapd sends review commands over your local Wi-Fi network to the Tapd Anki add-on. These requests may include your pairing token, device name during pairing, and review commands such as show answer, answer, undo, bury, or suspend. This traffic is used only to control Anki in real time and is not sent to Tapd servers.

Motion and Gesture Data

Gesture Mode uses Apple Watch motion sensors to detect wrist gestures. Motion readings are processed on the Watch for gesture detection and are not stored or transmitted to Tapd.

Anki Content

Tapd can display basic review state returned by the local Anki add-on, such as whether the reviewer is on the question or answer side and the active deck name. Tapd does not collect your cards, notes, deck content, or review history.

Children's Privacy

Tapd does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any other users.

Changes

If Tapd's privacy practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the contact method listed on the Tapd support page.
